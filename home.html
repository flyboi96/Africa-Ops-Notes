What this site is for

Africa Ops Notes is meant to consolidate country information that would otherwise live across scattered notes, messages, or gouge files. The objective is fast retrieval, consistent formatting, and easy maintenance.

  • One place to review country-specific planning information.
  • One structured notes format for all countries.
  • One protected workflow for trusted users to view or update data.

Recommended use

Use the map page for quick lookup and orientation. Use the notes page when you need the full country entry or when you are actively updating the underlying data. Keep entries concise, standardized, and operationally useful.
